## Lexora Vision

Lexora aims to solve the problem of unstructured, unreliable, and context-ignorant AI answers.

The system is designed to:
- Understand academic and professional context
- Generate answers in a fixed, reliable structure
- Adapt across universities, syllabi, and domains
- Include human verification where errors are costly

Lexora is not an ed-tech product.
It is a **platform-level intelligence system**.
